About Us

The story behind Aura Crystals

Who We Are

Aura Crystals UK is a small, passionate crystal shop based in the United Kingdom. We started because we believe that everyone deserves access to beautiful, ethically sourced crystals — without the high street markup.

Every single stone in our collection is personally hand-selected. We look at quality, energy, and beauty before anything reaches you.

Our Mission

We're on a mission to make crystal healing accessible, affordable, and authentic. Whether you're searching for something to help with anxiety, to decorate your home, or simply because a crystal called to you — we've got you.

Ethically Sourced

We only work with suppliers who share our values. We ask questions about where crystals come from, how they were mined, and who was involved. Because good energy starts at the source.
