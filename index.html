Who We Are

Aurora Crystals UK is a small, passionate crystal shop based in the United Kingdom. We started because we believe that everyone deserves access to beautiful, ethically sourced crystals — without the high street markup.

Our Mission

We're on a mission to make crystal healing accessible, affordable, and authentic. Whether you're searching for something to help with anxiety, to decorate your home, or simply because a crystal called to you — we've got you.
